James Kozak's art portfolio is colorful, creatvie and bold. It reflects the many textures and vibrancy his hometown of the Pittsburgh, PA.

His works include oil, acrylic, pastel and sculpture. His artwork captures the unique ethnicity of Pittsburgh's neighborhoods and it's beautiful, enveloping hills, rivers, and architecture.

The city's deep roots in the coal and steel industry along with it's transformation into a global technology center, provide for a rich pallete.

Jim graduated from St. Vincent College with additional art studies at Carnegie Mellon Univerisity.
