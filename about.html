James Kozak's art portfolio is colorful, creative, and bold. It reflects the vibrancy of his hometown of Pittsburgh, PA.

Art mediums include oil, acrylic, pastel and sculpture. His artwork works at capturing the unique ethnicity of Pittsburgh's neighborhoods and it's beautiful, enveloping hills, rivers, and architecture.

The city's deep roots in the coal and steel industry along with it's transformation into a global technology center, provide for a rich pallete.

Jim graduated from St. Vincent College with additional art studies at Carnegie Mellon Univerisity.
