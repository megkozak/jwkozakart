James Kozak's art portfolio is colorful, creatvie and bold. It reflects the many textures of the Pittsburgh landscape- his hometown.

His works include oil, acrylic, pastel and sculpture. He captures the unique ethnicity of Pittsburgh's neighborhoods and it's beatiful, enveloping landscape.

The city's deep roots in the coal and steel industry along with it's transformation into a global technology center, provide for a rich pallet.

Jim graduated from St. Vincent College with additional art studies at Carnegie Mellon Univerisity.
